Construction industry in Myanmar has been growing at an exponential rate of 20% since year 2010. Half of the market is contributed by residential sector at an approximate investment value of 1.5 Billion US Dollars, while industrial and commercial sectors combined to 20% and while the rest by infrastructure. Myanmar’s construction is still mainly concentrated in the residential sector fuelled by government housing plans and private commercial residential developments such as detached houses, apartments and high end condominiums driven by urbanization.

The use of construction materials in Myanmar from past to present has been functional based until third quarter of 2013 when more high rise hotels and buildings in Yangon have been built with more design. This growing development can be more commonly seen nowadays in restaurants, hotels and condominiums.
