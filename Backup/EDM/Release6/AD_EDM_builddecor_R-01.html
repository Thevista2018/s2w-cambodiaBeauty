The first Myanmar Build & Décor scheduled on 24-26 September at MEP ended very successfully. The three-day exhibition brought into Myanmar over hundred brands of international suppliers and manufacturers of building and construction materials, system work, interior design, furniture, décor and building and interior related product and service and was visited by almost 3000 professionals building and construction industry mostly from Myanmar.

Myanmar Build & Décor received an honor from H.E. U Nyan Tun Oo, Yangon Minister of Electric Power, Industry and Science & Technology to preside over its official opening on 24 September. Other VIPs at the grand opening and on the floor included UMFCCI Vice president, Myanmar Engineering Society Vice President, Myanmar A.G.T.I. Society President and Vice President, Myanmar Construction Entrepreneurs Association Vice president and Minister Counsellor (Commercial,office of Commercial Affairs from Royal Thai Embassy.

Organized by ICVEX, the exhibition held in partnership with TTF International, received an official support from UMFCCI, Myanmar Engineering Society, Myanmar A.G.T.I Society, and delivered an excellent business platform connecting international suppliers and manufacturers to doing business in Myanmar building and construction industry.
 

The second Myanmar Build & Décor will be held on 7-9 October, 2015 at the same centrally located venue, Myanmar Event Park (MEP) in Yangon. More information, www.myanmarbuilddecor.com, Tel: +662 713 3033
